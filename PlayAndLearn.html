Play And Learn

This was a project I started back in April 2021 when I first learned C++ and C. It was a team project me and one of my friend were the creators/developers for the project.

It is an eductaional game which aims at aiding students with their preparation for chemistry exams and tests.

This game contains couple of chemistry topics with different difficulty levels which a player can choose from and practice their concepts by playing the game.

One thing to keep in mind is that this game just works properly on the Linux Operating system.
